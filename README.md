# #93 Icon Mask Snap

A high-precision visual tool to verify and crop profile icons for major SNS platforms, ensuring your brand stays within the safe area.

## Core Philosophy
- **1 Tool / 1 Action**: Focuses entirely on the "last mile" of profile icon setup—perfect centering and safe-zone verification.
- **Direct Interaction**: No redundant buttons. Tap the viewer to load, drag to position, and zoom to fit.
- **Privacy First**: All image processing (including cropping) happens locally in your browser. No data ever leaves your device.

## Key Features
- **Smart Auto-Fit**: Automatically scales high-resolution photos (like iPhone Pro images) to fit the workspace upon loading.
- **Precision SNS Masks**: Toggle between specific safe-zone overlays for X (Twitter), Instagram, and YouTube.
- **Tactile Control**: Smooth drag-and-drop positioning and center-based zoom for intuitive framing.
- **High-Res Export**: Generates and downloads a 1024x1024px PNG of your perfectly framed icon with one tap.
- **User Guide Included**: Built-in guidance for finding your downloads on both iOS and Android.

## Design System
- **Background**: #fff
- **Main Color**: #333
- **Border Radius**: 15px (Consistent with Fridge Combo standards)
- **Immersive Mode**: The workspace darkens when a photo is loaded to highlight the active mask area.

## How to Use
1. **Load**: Tap the preview area to select your photo.
2. **Adjust**: Drag the photo to center it. Use the "Zoom Control" to scale.
3. **Verify**: Switch between SNS masks to ensure no critical part of your logo/face is cut off.
4. **Export**: Tap "DOWNLOAD PNG" to save your perfectly cropped icon.

## Platform Safe-Zone Ratios
- **General Circle**: 48% radius
- **X / Twitter**: 46.5% radius
- **Instagram**: 47.5% radius
- **YouTube**: 45.5% radius
